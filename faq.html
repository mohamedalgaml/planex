Frequently Asked Questions

What is Planex?

Planex is a platform that connects visionary founders with investors to create sustainable solutions for a better planet. We focus on renewable energy, sustainable agriculture, and eco-friendly infrastructure.

How do I join Planex?

You can join Planex by creating an account through the "Get Started" button on our homepage or by signing up via the "Sign Up / Log In" link in the navigation bar.

Who can become a member?

Anyone with a passion for sustainability—whether you’re a founder with an innovative idea or an investor looking to support impactful ventures—can become a member of Planex.

How do I contact support?

You can reach our support team by visiting the "Contact Us" page, accessible through the navigation menu under "Contact."

Is there a cost to join?

Joining Planex is free for founders to submit their ideas. Investors may have subscription options for premium features, details of which can be found after signing up.
