Our History

2023

Launch of Keep on Motivation (KM) YouTube

The Keep on Motivation (KM) journey began with the launch of the YouTube channel, aimed at delivering motivational content through longer-form videos. The channel featured inspiring talks, interviews with individuals who overcame challenges, and practical advice on personal growth. The focus on consistency and quality content helped attract a dedicated audience seeking encouragement and actionable motivation.

Birth of Planex

Planex was developed as a platform to promote global innovation and sustainability. The idea centers on bringing together creative minds to develop solutions that address pressing environmental and societal challenges. From its inception, Planex focused on refining its mission, exploring collaboration opportunities, and emphasizing action-driven innovation to make a tangible positive impact on the world.
